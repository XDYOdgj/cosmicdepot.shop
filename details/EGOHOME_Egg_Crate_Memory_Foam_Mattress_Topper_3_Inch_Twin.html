EGOHOME 3-Inch Memory Foam Mattress Topper with Gel Cooling & Lavender Scent

Elevate your sleep with a mattress topper that blends luxury and innovation.

Comfort Meets Innovation

This memory foam mattress topper from EGOHOME features a unique egg crate design that provides outstanding cushioning and support for a more restful night. The added gel cooling technology helps regulate your body temperature and maintain a cool sleep environment, making it ideal for hot sleepers or those who struggle with overheating during the night.

With its lavender scent, it adds a calming aroma that promotes relaxation and helps you drift off to sleep more easily. The open-cell structure of the foam offers excellent pressure relief, supporting your body and aligning your spine for a better sleep posture.

Versatile & Durable

This mattress pad is compatible with a variety of mattress types, including traditional, futon, and RV beds, making it a flexible solution for different lifestyles and living spaces. Whether you're looking to improve your current mattress or need a travel-friendly option, this topper fits a twin size of 38" x 75" seamlessly.

Part of EGOHOME's commitment to quality, the foam is CertiPUR-US certified, ensuring it's free from harmful chemicals and built to last for years of comfortable use. The combination of softness, support, and cooling technology makes this a must-have for anyone who values a good night's rest.
